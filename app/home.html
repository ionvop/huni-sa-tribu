ABOUT THE TRIBES
KAGAN
The Kagan people are known for their rich cultural heritage and traditional practices. They are masters of traditional music and dance ceremonies.
MANDAYA
The Mandaya tribe is one of the major indigenous groups in Mindanao, primarily found in Davao Oriental. They are masters of traditional music and dance ceremonies.
MANSAKA
The Mansaka people are skilled in various traditional crafts and have a deep connection with nature. They are masters of traditional music and dance ceremonies.
